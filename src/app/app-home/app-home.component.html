Welcome to HRCOC

The Highlands Ranch church of Christ seeks to be a loving congregation that proclaims the gospel of Jesus to all. Our desire is to help you find a deeper understanding of His word, and in all ways adhere to the teachings and commandments of Jesus, the apostles, and follow the pattern of the first century Church as is laid out in scripture.
